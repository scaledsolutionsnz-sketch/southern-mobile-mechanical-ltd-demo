About Southern Mobile Mechanical

Southland's machinery, kept running

Southern Mobile Mechanical is a Gore-based mobile outfit that services and repairs the gear Southland relies on — tractors, implements, trucks and heavy diesel. Being fully mobile means the workshop comes to you: the paddock, the yard or the roadside, so you're not tying up a ute and a driver getting a machine into town.

Run by Corey, the approach is straightforward — turn up when we say we will, sort the problem properly, and give you a clear picture of what it needed and why. No jargon, no surprises on the invoice.

  • Locally owned and based in Gore
  • Fully mobile — on-farm and on-site
  • Straight talk and clear pricing
