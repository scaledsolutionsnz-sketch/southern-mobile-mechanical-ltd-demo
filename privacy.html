Privacy Policy

How we handle the information you share with us. This is placeholder wording for the demo and isn't legal advice.

1. Who we are

Southern Mobile Mechanical Ltd is a mobile mechanical business based in Gore, Southland. This policy explains what personal information we collect and how we use it.

2. What we collect

  • Contact details you give us — name, phone number and email — when you book a callout or get in touch.
  • Details about your vehicle or machinery and the work required.
  • Any feedback you choose to send us through the website.

3. How we use it

We use your information to quote and carry out work, arrange callouts, invoice you, and keep in touch about your job. We don't sell your information, and we only share it where it's needed to complete the work (for example, ordering the right parts).

4. Website feedback

If you send feedback through our review page, it comes to us by email so we can respond. We use it to improve our service. We don't publish private feedback without your permission.

5. Storage and security

We keep your information only as long as we need it for the work and our records, and we take reasonable steps to keep it secure.

6. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you can ask to see the personal information we hold about you and request corrections. Get in touch and we'll help.
